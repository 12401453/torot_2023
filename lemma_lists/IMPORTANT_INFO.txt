The way this data is maintained is that the .xlsx spreadsheet-versions are what gets actively added to and corrected, then when I want to integrate them into my Autoreconstructor and SQLite databases I save the spreadsheets as .csv, overwriting the existing .csvs, then I run the node.js program to automatch lemmas between the ORV and CHU spreadsheets, then I take the resultant .csvs and run my autoreconstructor programs on them, then I copy and paste the body of the automatched csvs into the .xlsx spreadsheets so that the spreadsheets.
Therefore, when correcting things, correct the spreadsheets and don't worry about the .csvs.
