How can you tell imperfect occurrences of words like провожати and наслажати apart from jotated imperfects of прово[дз]ити and насладити? Do we risk losing some class 4 imperfects if we don't also reconstruct all of these class 54 secondary imperfects? This is quite a big problem because for instance there's only one occurence of крепляти (jat' btw) and it's an imperfect крѣплѧху that surely is instead from крѣпити.

My default assumption was always that class 54 secondary-imperfective counterparts to class 40 verbs like the above were developed off the back of the imperfects, i.e. the jotated imperfects date from a time BEFORE the perfective/imperfective had made its way into the DERIVATIONAL morphology (as opposed to the INFLECTIONAL morphology). That was one of the main reasons I objected to Andersen's claim that jotated-imperfects were newer than the non-jotated ones, because I had assumed that the development of jotated-imperfects preceded the development of full secondary-imperfective lemmas, i.e. I assumed that the timeline went something like:
    1.) *privoditi = "bring"[NO ASPECT], *privodixъ "I brung"[PRETERITE], *privoђǢaxъ "I was bringing"[IMPERFECT]
    2.) *privodixъ "I brung"[PERFECTIVE PRETERITE], *privoђǢaxъ "I was bringing"[IMPERFECTIVE]
    3.) *privoditi[PERFECTIVE] <--- *privodixъ[PERFECTIVE], but *privoђǢaxъ[IMPERFECTIVE] due to the aspect-mismatch no longer gets associated with *privoditi[PERFECTIVE] and so instead a secondary-imperfective infinitive based on the perceived imperfect-stem is innovated: *privoђǢ-ti --> *privoђǢjǫ

But I don't know really anything about the history of Slavic aspect (apart from when I had to read your Dag Haug article, which I may have misunderstood), so that whole assumption might be wrong.

The point is, we don't get Russian secondary-imperfectives like **наставять, we instead always get jotated forms like наставлять or помышлять, so if the assumption that jotated-imperfects were the source of this type of Russian secondary-imperfective, then non-jotated imperfects HAVE to be late analogical formations from AFTER the aspectual distinctions had moved from INFLECTIONAL to DERIVATIONAL morphology, because otherwise we would expect non-jotated imperfects to have served as the model on which to build secondary-imperfectives, but we don't get such **наставять or **помыслять types in Russian.
